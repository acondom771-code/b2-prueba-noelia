1. INTRODUCTION

Sustainable Travel Guide&nbsp;

This project is designed as a learning situation in which students will create a sustainable travel guide about English-speaking countries.

Students will use digital tools such as Canva, Genially or VistaCreate to design an interactive and visually attractive guide.

The project promotes creativity, digital competence, collaboration and environmental awareness.
