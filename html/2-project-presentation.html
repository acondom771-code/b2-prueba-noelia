LEARNING SITUATION (3º ESO)

2. PROJECT PRESENTATION

PRESENTATION

This project is aimed at students of 3º ESO. Students will explore different English-speaking countries and design a sustainable travel guide. The main goal is to develop communicative, digital and social skills while promoting sustainability and responsible tourism. The project follows the principles of Universal Design for Learning (UDL), ensuring inclusion and accessibility for all students.
